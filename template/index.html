About EEG-BCI

EEG-BCI (Electroencephalogram Based Brain Computer Interface) is basically a analyser webapp used Machine Learning and Deep Learning algorithms at backend. With the help of this analyser one can analyse the emotions with the help of Electroencephalograms (EEG) signals. The dataset is used to train the model is "EEG Brainwave Dataset: Feeling Emotions". Electroencephalograms are the mixture of Neuroscience and Electronics Science. Electroencephalography is a complex signal due to it reflects the cortical electrical activity and can require several years of experience and training also advanced signal processing and feature extraction system to be correctly interpret. Recently, Machine Learning and Deep Learning has shown great availability in helping make sense of EEG signal due to its capacity to learn fast and effective feature representation from raw data. Whether ML & DL truly presents advantages as compared to traditional EEG signal processing approaches. In this project we used some popular Machine Learning Algorithms and Deep Neural Networks to analyse the EEG signal and classify the emotions related to that signal. We used simple technique to analyse the signal in better way by extracting the stastical properties of the signal. This helps us to reduction in the complexty of EEG signal analysis.We got nearly 99% accuracy. This will help to analyse the EEG signal effectively and fast. Also, with the help of this analyzer one can make a Brain Computer Interface technology in economical way due to increase in the quality of EEG signal system analysis.
